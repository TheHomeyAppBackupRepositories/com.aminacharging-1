Enhance your smart home with amina S for a stable charging experience. Seamlessly integrate amina S with Homey for simplified home control and cost-effective charging. amina S is the small, fast, and smart charger that gives you complete control over charging speed and energy consumption. With its 6 m lightweight, flexible and fixed cable, you can charge up to 22kW.

Key features:
- Start and stop charging
- Set charging current
- Realtime energy consumption
- Charging history
- Alarm notifications

In addition to key functionality, you can combine amina S with other devices to build flows for load balancing, price optimised charging and solar energy utilisation. Discover all possibilities with amina below.

For the best experience in Homey amina S should be on firmware version 1.8.7 or later.
